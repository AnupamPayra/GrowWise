Step-by-Step Admission Guide

Follow this proven roadmap to maximize your college admission success

Research & Planning

Junior Year - Fall

Start your college journey by researching schools, understanding admission requirements, and creating a strategic plan tailored to your goals.

ESSENTIAL CHECKLIST

  • Create list of 15-20 potential colleges
  • Research admission requirements for each
  • Attend college fairs and virtual sessions
  • Start building your activity resume

EXPERT TIPS

  • Use spreadsheets to track deadlines and requirements
  • Visit campuses early to get a feel for fit
  • Connect with current students and alumni

Academic Preparation

Junior Year - Spring

Focus on maintaining strong grades, taking challenging courses, and preparing for standardized tests to build a competitive academic profile.

ESSENTIAL CHECKLIST

  • Maintain GPA above 3.5 (ideally 3.7+)
  • Take SAT/ACT practice tests
  • Register for standardized tests
  • Enroll in AP/IB/Dual enrollment courses

EXPERT TIPS

  • Take the harder test prep route - it pays off
  • Plan for 2-3 test attempts if needed
  • Senior year grades still matter - don't slack off

Extracurricular Excellence

Throughout High School

Develop meaningful extracurricular involvement that demonstrates leadership, passion, and commitment to causes you care about.

ESSENTIAL CHECKLIST

  • Lead 2-3 activities you're passionate about
  • Volunteer consistently (100+ hours)
  • Pursue summer programs or internships
  • Document all achievements and impact

EXPERT TIPS

  • Quality over quantity - depth matters more
  • Show progression and increasing responsibility
  • Connect activities to your intended major

Essay Writing

Summer Before Senior Year

Craft compelling personal statements and supplemental essays that showcase your unique voice, experiences, and fit with each college.

ESSENTIAL CHECKLIST

  • Write Common App personal statement
  • Draft supplemental essays for each school
  • Get feedback from teachers and counselors
  • Revise multiple times for perfection

EXPERT TIPS

  • Show, don't tell - use specific examples
  • Each essay should reveal something new
  • Research each school's values and culture

Recommendations

Early Senior Year

Secure strong recommendation letters from teachers, counselors, and mentors who know you well and can speak to your character and abilities.

ESSENTIAL CHECKLIST

  • Ask recommenders 6-8 weeks in advance
  • Provide resume and essay drafts
  • Choose teachers from core subjects
  • Follow up politely and send thank you notes

EXPERT TIPS

  • Choose teachers who've seen your growth
  • Provide specific examples for recommenders
  • Waive your right to see recommendations

Application Submission

Fall Senior Year

Submit polished applications well before deadlines, ensuring all components are complete and error-free.

ESSENTIAL CHECKLIST

  • Submit applications 1-2 weeks early
  • Double-check all requirements
  • Pay application fees or request waivers
  • Send official transcripts and test scores

EXPERT TIPS

  • Keep copies of everything you submit
  • Proofread one final time before submitting
  • Confirm receipt of all application materials
