Exam Details

In India, entrance exams are broadly categorized into Engineering exams (like JEE, BITSAT, state CETs), Medical exams (like NEET for MBBS, BDS, Pharmacy, Nursing), Management and Commerce exams (like CUET, IPMAT, CA Foundation, CS, CMA), Arts, Humanities, and Design exams (like CUET, NID, NIFT, TISS BAT), Law exams (like CLAT, AILET, LSAT), Defence exams (like NDA, Airforce, Navy entries), Diploma/Vocational exams after 10th (like Polytechnic, ITI), and Scholarship/Talent exams (like NTSE, Olympiads, KVPY).
