Official Website

Eligibility

  • Should have passed Class 12th or equivalent examination.
  • No age limit for candidates.
  • Max 3 consecutive years allowed.

Exam Pattern

Two papers: Paper 1 (B.E./B.Tech), Paper 2 (B.Arch/B.Planning).

Preparation

Focus on Physics, Chemistry, Maths. Practice mocks regularly.
