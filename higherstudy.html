Honours Career Section

An Honours degree (like B.A. Hons, B.Sc. Hons, B.Com. Hons, etc.) can open many specialized career paths compared to a general degree, since it usually involves deeper subject knowledge, research, and sometimes a thesis/project. The exact path depends on your field of study. Your journey to a rewarding honours career starts here!

English Honours

B.A.EnglishHonours is a 3-year undergraduate degree, focuses onEnglish literature, language,criticism, andwriting. It goes deeper than general English courses — not just grammar or communication, but also poetry, drama, novels, literary theory, linguistics, and cultural studies.

Multiple specializations: M.A in English literature, Content Writing, B.Ed.(Bachelor of Education).
Work in: Education & Research, Creative & Cultural fields, Goverment & Competitive Exams.
Required: 10+2(any stream, but usually with English as a subject).
