Career Choices After 10th – Streams & Courses

Science

Focuses on subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Maths, and Biology. Opens pathways to engineering, medical, research, IT, and pure sciences. Best for analytical and problem-solving careers.

Commerce

Deals with business, finance, economics, and management. Leads to careers in accounting, banking, entrepreneurship, corporate law, and management studies.

Arts

Includes subjects like History, Political Science, Psychology, Sociology, Literature, Fine Arts, and Journalism. Ideal for creative, social science, media, and civil service careers.

Vocational

Practical and skill-based courses (like Fashion Designing, Animation, Tourism, IT, Paramedical). Provides job-ready skills in specific industries.

Higher Study

Technical programs after 10th or 12th in fields like Engineering, IT, Mechanical, Civil, and Electronics. Good for early entry into technical jobs or further studies (B.Tech, etc.).

Professional

Specialized career-focused programs like CA, CS, CMA, Law, Design, Hotel Management, and Aviation. Prepares students directly for professional roles in industry.
